what is ROS and how to connect with Artifical intelligence

ROS, or Robot Operating System, is an open-source framework designed for robotics development. Integrating AI with ROS allows robots to perform more intelligent and autonomous tasks.

basic knowledge of neural network and there uses

A neural network is a computational model that is designed based on the functions of animal nerve systems, especially the brain to process information. It consists of layers of interconnected nodes (neurons). These networks are used to recognize patterns, make decisions as well as for accomplishing complex tasks through learning from data.

Large Language Model (LLM)

Large Language Models (LLMs) have revolutionized the natural language processing by excelling in tasks such as text generation, translation, summarization and question answering. Despite their impressive capabilities, these models may not always be suitable for specific tasks or domains due to compatibility issues.

what is Generative AI?

Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can create new content or data, such as text, images, music, and more, by learning patterns from existing data. These models can generate human-like text, produce realistic images, create original music, and even design new products.

what is machine learning?

Machine learning (ML) is a subfield of artificial intelligence (AI) that involves the development of algorithms and statistical models that enable computers to learn from and make predictions or decisions based on data. Unlike traditional programming, where a computer follows explicit instructions to perform a task, machine learning allows computers to identify patterns and infer rules from data to improve their performance on a specific task over time.
